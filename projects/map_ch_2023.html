Project Overview

This section showcases the visuals I produced during the 30-day map challenge in 2023. Although I missed 2 days, I extremely enjoyed the process of thinking through the ideas and finally implementing them. This challenge significantly sharpened my visualization and analytical skills, expanded my familiarity with open data platforms, and deepened my understanding of effective geospatial communication. It also helped build a global network of mapmakers and fostered a deeper appreciation for the art and science of cartography.
